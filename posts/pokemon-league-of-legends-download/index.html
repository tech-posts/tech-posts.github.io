The World of Champions#

In this ROM hack, the world is inhabited by creatures called champions. These champions are controlled by summoners, who have the power to control and command them. The champions and summoners once put an end to the Rune Wars, but there are still champions who seek to overthrow the summoners and rule Valerian. As a summoner, it is your role to rid the world of evil and prevent major wars from erupting again.

A Journey Begins#

To embark on your adventure, you must first create your character. Choose whether you want to play as a boy or a girl, and then provide a nickname for your character. This will be your identity as you navigate through the world of champions.

Exploring Valerian#

As you enter the game, you’ll find yourself in Valerian, a land full of wonders and challenges. Battle Mechanics#

In the ROM hack, battles are reminiscent of Pokemon battles. You’ll have the opportunity to control champions and engage in strategic combat. Champion Centers and Shops#

Throughout your journey, you’ll come across champion centers where Nurse Akali will heal your champions. These locations are essential for restoring your champions’ health after battles. Additionally, you’ll encounter shops where you can purchase items to aid you in battle.
