In this Kodi tutorial, we will guide you through the process of installing the ShowBox Arize Kodi Addon. ShowBox is a popular streaming application that allows users to watch movies and TV shows for free. By adding ShowBox to Kodi, you can enhance your streaming experience and access a wide range of content. Follow the step-by-step instructions below to install ShowBox Arize Kodi Addon.

Step 1: Access File Manager#

  1. From the Kodi home page, navigate to the gear icon located at the top left corner of the screen.
  2. Scroll down and click on “File Manager” from the options.

Step 2: Add Source#

  1. In the File Manager, select “Add Source”, which is usually the last option in the left column.
  2. Click on “None” to enter the source URL.
  3. Enter the URL mentioned in the video description or the one provided by the repository.
  4. Press “OK” to proceed.
  5. Give a name for this source and press “OK” again.

Step 3: Install Repository#

  1. Return to the Kodi home screen and navigate to “Add-ons”.
  2. Click on the box icon located at the top left corner of the screen.
  3. Select “Install from zip file”.
  4. Look for the advance source that you just added and choose the repository zip file.
  5. Wait for the repository to install. You will see a notification in the top right corner once the installation is complete.

Step 4: Install Addon#

  1. From the Kodi home screen, go to “Add-ons”.
  2. Click on “Install from repository”.
  3. Look for the repository you just installed and select it.
  4. Navigate to “Video Add-ons” and locate ShowBox Arize Kodi Addon.
  5. Click on “Install” to add the addon.
  6. Wait for the notification in the top right corner, indicating that the installation is successful.

Step 5: Access the Addon#

  1. Go back to the Kodi home screen and access the “Add-ons” tab.
  2. Here, you will find the recently installed ShowBox Arize Kodi Addon.
  3. Start exploring the addon and enjoy free streaming of movies and TV shows.

Congratulations! You have successfully installed ShowBox Arize Kodi Addon. Now you can take advantage of its features and access a wide range of content for your entertainment needs.
