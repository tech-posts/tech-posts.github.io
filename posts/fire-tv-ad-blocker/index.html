The Problem with Ads on Streaming Sites#

Before we dive into the solution, let’s explore why ads on streaming sites can be so bothersome. These sites are notorious for hosting numerous pop-ups and video-based ads that hinder the navigation process. As you try to click on links or move around the page, you are constantly bombarded with different adverts. Refreshing the page only adds floating adverts that take up most of the screen, making it even more frustrating to browse and enjoy your favorite sports or streaming content.

The Solution: Blocking Ads with Silk Browser#

Imagine a browsing experience without any ads. By using the Silk Browser on your Fire TV, you can effortlessly block ads on any website. Whether you have a third-generation Fire TV Cube or any other Amazon device that supports the Silk Browser, you can follow this step-by-step process to enjoy an ad-free browsing experience.

Step-by-Step Guide to Block Ads on Fire TV#

  1. Install Silk Browser: If you haven’t already, make sure you have the Silk Browser installed on your Fire TV device. If you’re using an Android TV device, you can also side load the Silk Browser onto it.

  2. Open Silk Browser: Launch the Silk Browser on your Fire TV device.

  3. Access Settings: Press the context key, which is represented by three lines, to bring up the menu. Scroll down and click on “Settings.”

  4. Navigate to Privacy and Security: Keep scrolling down in the Settings menu until you find the “Privacy and Security” option. Click on it to proceed.

  5. Turn on Safe Browsing: Enable “Safe Browsing” to automatically block websites classified as hosting malware or potentially malicious content.

  6. Enable Do Not Track: Activate “Do Not Track” to reduce the amount of tracking by websites as you browse.

  7. Use Secure DNS: The key step to blocking ads is using a secure DNS server. Click on the option that says “Use Secure DNS.”

  8. Add Custom DNS Provider: Choose the option to add another provider and enter the following custom URL in the correct format: https://dns.adguard-dns.com/DNS-query.

  9. Verify the Provider: Validate the URL by clicking on “Next.” If there are any errors, ensure the URL is entered correctly and try again.

  10. Enjoy Ad-Free Browsing: Once the DNS provider is set up, you can now experience ad-free browsing on your Fire TV device. Websites that attempt to serve ads will be blocked by the AdGuard DNS servers.

  11. Stream Without Ads: Visit your favorite streaming websites and enjoy a seamless streaming experience without any interruptions. No more ads, pop-ups, or video-based adverts. Click on the play button and start streaming in HD quality without any delays.

Additional Benefits of Ad Blocking#

Blocking ads not only enhances your browsing experience but also provides additional benefits:

  • Increased Security: By blocking ads, you decrease your exposure to potentially harmful malware and malicious content.

  • Improved Privacy: Enabling “Do Not Track” reduces the ability of websites to track your online activity, giving you more privacy while browsing.

  • Faster Loading Times: Without ads, web pages load faster, allowing you to access content more quickly.
