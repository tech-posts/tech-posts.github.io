How to Download YouTube Videos - A Comprehensive Guide

Downloading YouTube videos can be a great way to enjoy your favorite content offline, whether it’s on your laptop, desktop, iPhone, or Android phone. In this article, we will explore the easiest and most legal way to download YouTube videos using YouTube Premium. We will also discuss the benefits of a premium membership and provide a step-by-step guide on how to access and download videos. So let’s dive in!

The Benefits of YouTube Premium#

Before we get into the details of downloading YouTube videos, let’s take a look at what you get with a YouTube Premium membership.

  1. Ad-Free Experience: With YouTube Premium, you no longer have to worry about pesky ads interrupting your videos. Say goodbye to pre-roll ads, integrated ads, and display ads on the sides of the video. This provides a seamless viewing experience.

  2. Free Movies and TV Shows: YouTube Premium offers a large collection of free movies and TV shows. Once you become a premium member, you can enjoy these shows and movies without any ads.

  3. Support Your Favorite Creators: As a premium member, when you watch a video, the creator receives a portion of your membership fee. This means that even though you don’t see ads, you still contribute to the success of your favorite content creators.

  4. Background Play: YouTube Premium allows you to listen to music or watch videos while switching between apps or even with your screen turned off. This is especially useful for mobile users who want to multitask.

  5. Offline Access: One of the key features of YouTube Premium is the ability to download videos for offline viewing. You can download videos on both desktop and mobile, and watch them later without an internet connection. Just make sure to connect to the internet at least once every 30 days to keep your downloaded content active.

  6. YouTube Music Premium: Along with YouTube Premium, you also get access to YouTube Music, which is YouTube’s version of Spotify. You can enjoy ad-free music streaming and even download your favorite songs for offline listening.

How to Get YouTube Premium#

To get YouTube Premium, follow these steps:

  1. Go to youtube.com and click on “YouTube Premium” in the left-hand menu.
  2. Explore the details of the premium membership to see if it’s worth it for you.
  3. Sign up for a monthly plan, which costs around $12 per month, or consider the annual plan for around $120 per year.
  4. If you live outside the United States, check the pricing in your market.
  5. You can also opt for the family option, which costs $18 per month and allows you to share your premium membership with up to five other people.
  6. Click on “Try it free” to start a one-month trial. After the trial, you will be charged according to your chosen plan.
  7. To save money, click on the “x” in the top left corner to receive a 30% discount on your first paid month.

Remember, if you sign up on your mobile device, such as an iPhone, the price may be higher due to the app store’s cut. It’s best to sign up through the website for the most affordable option.

Downloading YouTube Videos#

Now that you have a YouTube Premium membership, you can download videos for offline viewing. Here’s how:

  1. Visit the YouTube page of the video you want to download.
  2. Click on the new “Download” button on the video page.
  3. The video will start downloading, and you can find all your downloaded videos under the “Downloads” section.
  4. If you want to remove a downloaded video, simply click on the ellipsis and select “Delete.”
  5. Adjust your download settings by clicking on “Download Settings” and choosing the resolution for all your downloads.

Please note that the maximum resolution available for downloading is 1080p, which is still suitable for most on-the-go viewing. Unfortunately, 4K quality is not yet available for downloads.

On mobile, you can access your downloaded videos by clicking on “Libraries” in the bottom right corner. From there, you can enjoy your offline content and manage your downloads.

Accessing YouTube Music#

As a YouTube Premium member, you also have access to YouTube Music. Here’s how:

  1. On youtube.com, click on “YouTube Music” in the left-hand menu.
  2. Explore the ad-free catalog of music available to you.
  3. You can also download the dedicated YouTube Music app on your mobile device for easy access to the entire music catalog.
  4. Enjoy uninterrupted music streaming and take advantage of the option to listen to music offline.

Is YouTube Premium Worth It?#

YouTube Premium offers a range of benefits, such as an ad-free experience, access to free movies and TV shows, and the ability to download videos and stream music offline. The monthly cost of around $12 may be worth it for avid YouTube users who want an enhanced viewing experience and support their favorite creators.

Compared to other music streaming services like Spotify, YouTube Premium offers similar pricing but includes ad-free YouTube and video downloading capabilities. If you already have a Spotify subscription, it might be worth considering a switch to YouTube Premium.

To manage your YouTube Premium membership, click on your profile picture in the top right corner and select “Purchases and Memberships.” From there, you can view your membership details and even deactivate your subscription if needed.

Conclusion#

In conclusion, YouTube Premium provides a hassle-free and legal way to download YouTube videos and enjoy an ad-free experience. With additional perks like access to free movies, offline music streaming, and support for content creators, it offers excellent value for its monthly price. If you’re a frequent YouTube user looking for an enhanced viewing and listening experience, YouTube Premium is definitely worth considering. Start your free trial today and explore the world of ad-free YouTube!
