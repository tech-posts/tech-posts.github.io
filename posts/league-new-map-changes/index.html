Summoner's Rift Preview: A Detailed Look at the Upcoming Map Changes in League of Legends

July 18, 2023 · 5 min · 950 words · Jayson Stump
Table of Contents

  • Enhanced Orientation and Unique Themes

Welcome to the Summoner’s Rift Preview! By now, you’ve probably heard about the highly anticipated update to Summoner’s Rift in League of Legends. In this article, we’ll delve into the details of what Riot Games aims to accomplish with these changes. From improved readability to enhanced visuals, let’s explore the upcoming alterations and their impact on gameplay.

Improving Clarity and Readability#

One of the primary goals of the map update is to enhance clarity and make gameplay information more readable. Riot Games has implemented various strategies to achieve this objective. One such approach is controlling color saturation and level of detail in the environment. The idea behind this is to ensure that players’ attention is focused on vital elements such as the UI, champion abilities, summoner spells, champions, minions, and monsters, rather than being distracted by the environment itself.

To demonstrate the impact of these changes, let’s take a look at a comparison between the current Summoner’s Rift and the updated version. The navigation mesh, which determines where players can and cannot move, is now less obscured by the environment. This allows for better visibility of passable spaces, flashable walls, and possible ward placements. The updated map design enables players to make more informed decisions and react swiftly in crucial moments.

Enhanced Orientation and Unique Themes#

In addition to improved clarity, Riot Games has also aimed to make it easier for players to orient themselves on the map. Each jungle quadrant now boasts a unique theme, allowing players to know their location at a glance. For instance, Barun, the powerful elemental dragon, serves as an orientation point in the jungle. The destruction around Barun’s pit radiates outward, creating a stark visual contrast that helps players identify their position.

In the West quadrant, vibrant plant life and split rock formations indicate the influence of Barun’s emergence. On the other hand, in the East quadrant, the untouched beauty of the forest remains intact, emphasizing the diversity of the different jungle areas. Despite both quadrants being home to the blue buff objective, the visual distinctions make it clear which quadrant players are in.

Evolving Art Style and Enhanced Monster Visuals#

Summoner’s Rift is a beloved location in League of Legends, and Riot Games aims to make it a place where players can continue to enjoy the game for years to come. With this update, a handcrafted art style has been chosen to ensure that the map is not only visually appealing but also able to evolve alongside the rest of the game. The new art style adds a touch of fantasy and brings the jungle monsters to life with more personality.

Jungle monsters now feel more at home on the Rift and better communicate their power levels. For example, the Red Buff and Brambleback are visually striking, with intimidating roars that warn players of their strength. The Crugs, on the other hand, adopt a defensive stance, indicating their tanky style and damage-soaking abilities. Each jungle camp has been given individualized characteristics, such as the Murk Wolves emerging eagerly from their cave and the Grump adjusting to its new weight class.

Unique Bases and Minion Distinctions#

The two bases on Summoner’s Rift have also undergone significant changes to provide a more distinctive visual experience. While the architecture of both bases remains similar, their differences lie in their animal-inspired motifs. The red base features an owl motif and curvilinear shapes, while the blue base draws inspiration from stags and incorporates blockier shapes. These unique design choices give each base its own identity and add depth to the map.

Even the minions have received a visual revamp. The red minions now wield axes, matching the curvy shape language of their base, while the blue minions continue to adhere to their square traditions, swinging mighty war hammers. These distinctions, combined with brighter color saturation, contribute to improved readability during intense battles.

Elevating Dragon and Baron’s Enigma#

To elevate the importance of dragon and Baron Nashor, the map update adds an element of ceremony to these crucial objectives. Dragon now appears menacing and imposing, fiercely guarding its territory. Its new abilities reflect the devastating damage it can unleash, adding strategic depth to fights over this objective. Likewise, Baron Nashor has received a redesigned appearance, depicting its status as the toughest boss on the Rift.

The updated Baron Nashor is accompanied by a kit redesign, enhancing its abilities and attack patterns. These improvements result in a less random experience, allowing teams to make informed decisions on when to engage in combat in pursuit of the powerful Baron buff.

Ongoing Development and Community Interaction#

While the map update is a significant undertaking, it is important to note that it is still a work in progress. Riot Games plans to continue refining and iterating on the feedback received from players during the Public Beta Environment (PBE) testing phase. Bugs will be squashed, performance will be improved, and player feedback will be taken into account to ensure a smooth and enjoyable experience on all machines.

Throughout this process, players can keep track of the map’s evolution by visiting the Summoner’s Rift Hub. Here, they can engage in ongoing conversations with Riot Games through dev blogs, Q&A sessions, and patch notes, which detail the latest changes and improvements.

In conclusion, Riot Games’ Summoner’s Rift Preview offers a glimpse into the exciting changes coming to League of Legends’ most iconic map. The update focuses on enhancing clarity, improving readability, and adding visual depth to the gameplay experience. Players can look forward to exploring the revamped Summoner’s Rift, where strategic decisions and immersive battles await. Stay tuned for more updates as Riot Games continues to refine and polish the map for an unforgettable gaming experience.
