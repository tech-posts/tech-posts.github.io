The Declining State of For Honor in 2023: A Disappointing Game Review

Note: This article is a transcription and analysis of a Youtube video titled “The Absolute State of For Honor in 2023”. The channel name is Romender.

For Honor, a once-promising game, has failed to live up to its potential. In this article, we will explore the current state of For Honor in 2023 and delve into why it has become a disappointing experience for players.

Introduction: A Prophecy of Victory#

The video starts with a sense of excitement as the narrator mentions a prophecy of victory exclusive to “hawkos,” referring to the players. However, this enthusiasm quickly wanes as the narrator expresses his frustration with the self-indulgent nature of the game and begs for a calmer approach.

An Overwhelming Amount of Lore#

One of the key points raised in the video is the excess of lore in For Honor. According to the narrator, the game doesn’t require such an intricate backstory. He argues that For Honor is simply a funny sword game, and the abundance of lore is unnecessary. Players prefer to focus on the action rather than getting lost in the lore.

Gameplay Analysis#

The video also showcases some gameplay moments, highlighting both victories and defeats. The narrator displays frustration at certain moments and is surprised by the unpredictability of his opponents’ actions. He conveys his thoughts on different playstyles and strategies employed by other players in the game.

Frustrations with Balancing and Strategies#

Throughout the video, the narrator expresses frustration with the imbalance in the game and lack of variety in strategies employed by opponents. He mentions the prevalence of Black Prior, a character who can effectively counter dodge attacks. The narrator reiterates that certain characters seem to play in similar ways, reducing the overall experience.

Dominion: Group Battles and Breaking News#

The video discusses the game mode Dominion, which involves group battles. The narrator questions the game’s logic in discouraging group fighting when the mode is explicitly designed for it. He suggests that the news of his victory in this game mode should be considered “breaking news.”

Moments of Victory and Defeat#

The video highlights both victorious and defeated moments, emphasizing how players can experience a range of emotions. The narrator displays a sense of satisfaction when he successfully executes a strategy or surprises his opponents. Conversely, he expresses disappointment and frustration when defeated.

Aggressive Playstyles and Intimidation#

The narrator mentions encounters with players who adopt an aggressive playstyle, staying close to their opponents throughout the match. This aggressive approach seems to unnerve the narrator, who decides to mimic the strategy himself to gain an advantage. The video highlights the effect of intimidation on the gameplay experience.

Final Verdict: A Disappointing Game#

In conclusion, the video offers a scathing review of For Honor in 2023. The narrator deems it a “crappy game” and advises players to stay away from it. It is clear that the game has failed to provide an enjoyable and balanced experience for the narrator, leading to his frustration and disappointment.

Conclusion#

For Honor’s current state in 2023 leaves much to be desired. The excessive lore, lack of balance, and repetitive playstyles have contributed to its decline. The video’s review sheds light on the frustrations experienced by players, providing insight into the state of the game. It remains to be seen whether For Honor will be able to regain its former glory or if it will continue to disappoint its player base.
