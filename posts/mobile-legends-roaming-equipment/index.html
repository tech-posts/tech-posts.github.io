Roaming in Mobile Legends: Bang Bang (MLBB) is a crucial role that requires players to move around the map and provide support to all lanes. In this article, we will explore the key aspects of playing as a Roamer, including the important tasks, item builds, and rotation strategies. If you want to improve your roaming skills and make a significant impact on the game, continue reading.

Understanding the Role of a Roamer#

As a Roamer in MLBB, your main objective is to assist all lanes rather than focusing on a specific lane. This involves various tasks and responsibilities that can greatly influence the outcome of a match. Let’s delve into the key points to consider when playing as a Roamer.

Clearing Bushes and Providing Vision#

One of the first tasks as a Roamer is to clear bushes and provide vision for your team. By doing so, you can prevent your teammates from getting ambushed by the enemy. Vision also helps side laners avoid ganks, and it enables your jungler and Mage to determine which lane to rotate or gank. Clearing bushes and providing information about the enemy’s location is essential in preventing your teammates from getting outnumbered and defeated.

Protecting Teammates and Giving Backup#

Protecting teammates is a crucial aspect of being a Roamer. While it may not always be possible to be present in every lane, you must read the situation and check the mini-map consistently. It is vital to be there for most team fights and provide assistance when your teammates need help initiating or setting up a fight. Utilize your skills to set up fights, particularly when contesting objectives such as buffs, the Lord, or the Turtle. However, it is crucial to assess the risk involved and avoid fights that are too risky and likely to result in a loss.

Ganking Enemies#

Ganking enemies, especially side laners, in the early stages of the game is another key task for a Roamer. Coordinate with your teammates to secure kills and help them win their respective lanes. However, be cautious of enemy backup and learn how to gank effectively without falling into traps or getting counter-ganked. Understanding the art of ganking can significantly contribute to your team’s success.

Stopping Ganks in the Side Lanes#

As a Roamer, you can play a vital role in preventing ganks in the side lanes, particularly for gold laners. By positioning yourself strategically and anticipating the enemy’s movements, you can deter the opposing team from successfully ganking your allies. Being present in their jungle areas can disrupt the enemy’s positioning and protect your marksman from being overwhelmed by multiple enemies.

Essential Roaming Items#

When playing as a Roamer in MLBB, there are four primary roaming items to consider: Conceal, Courage, Flicker, and Awe Mask. Each item provides unique effects and benefits.

1. Conceal#

Conceal grants you and nearby teammates a concealment effect and extra movement speed for a short duration. This item is especially useful for initiators like Diggie, Kufra, and Atlas. By activating Conceal during a fight, you make it harder for the enemies to spot you and gain an advantage with increased movement speed.

2. Courage#

Courage gives you and nearby teammates additional physical attack, magic power, and attack speed. This item is suitable for tanky Roamers who excel in the late game and can sustain longer in team fights. The more time your teammates spend near you, the more they benefit from the effect of Courage.

3. Flicker#

Flicker grants you the ability to teleport a short distance, which can be a game-changer in certain situations. Its versatility makes it useful for any Roamer, allowing you to engage, disengage, or reposition yourself during fights. Its short cooldown and quick mobility make Flicker an essential roaming item.

4. Awe Mask#

Awe Mask increases your damage output by dealing extra hybrid damage to enemy heroes when they are below 35% HP. Damage dealer type Roamers can utilize Awe Mask to unleash even more damage and harass enemies effectively. It is an excellent item for Roamers who want to secure kills and create pressure.

Mastering Rotation Strategies#

Rotation is a crucial aspect of being an effective Roamer. Moving around the map without a plan is a common mistake that can hinder your team’s success. Let’s discuss some rotation strategies for Roamers.

Early Game Rotation#

In the early game, your main focus should be to ensure that your jungler isn’t being invaded. Support them in securing buffs and check bushes to detect any potential threats. Afterward, head to the gold lane and try to secure kills or force the enemy to recall. When the Turtle is about to respawn, coordinate with your teammates to secure it while maintaining control of the surrounding bushes.

Mid Game Rotation#

During the mid game, it is crucial to follow your jungler’s rotation or assist your gold laner if they are struggling. Always check the bushes that your teammates will pass through and provide backup during fights. If your team is in a winning condition, take advantage of the lead by harassing the enemy in their jungle area and set up fights near objectives like the Lord or Turtle.

Late Game Rotation#

In the late game, stick with your Mage or gold laner and ensure that they have backup near bushes. Initiate team fights at the right time and communicate effectively with your team. Be wary of enemies who may invade your jungle or distract your jungler. Heroes like Natalia, Franco, and Hilda tend to distract and disrupt your team’s progress. Stick with your jungler until the first turtle and block the enemies from getting close.

By implementing these rotation strategies, you can maximize your impact as a Roamer and contribute to your team’s victory.

In conclusion, playing as a Roamer in MLBB comes with a set of responsibilities and tasks that can greatly influence the outcome of a match. From clearing bushes and providing vision to protecting teammates, ganking enemies, and mastering rotation strategies, there is a lot to consider. By understanding the role, utilizing the right roaming items, and implementing effective rotation tactics, you can become a formidable Roamer and lead your team to victory.

Remember, practice and experience are key to mastering this role. Keep honing your skills, communicate with your teammates, and always stay aware of the map to make better decisions. Happy roaming!
