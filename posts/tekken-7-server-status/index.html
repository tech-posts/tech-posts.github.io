Tekken 7 - Mastering Knockdown Recovery Techniques

Introduction#

In this Tekken 7 tutorial, we will explore one of the most frustrating aspects of the game that often goes unexplained - what to do when you’re knocked down. As a relative newcomer to the Tekken series, I understand the confusion and stress that comes with being in this vulnerable position. In this guide, I will share the techniques and strategies I have learned to help you navigate the chaotic world of knockdown recovery. Let’s dive in!

Understanding Your Options#

When you find yourself knocked down in Tekken 7, you have several options for recovery. Let’s take a closer look at each of them:

  1. Low Kick - By pressing 3, you can perform a low kick, which deals damage and can catch your opponent off guard.
  2. Medium Kick - Pressing the right kick button executes a medium kick, also known as a middle TAC. This move is more versatile and can create opportunities for counterattacks.
  3. Getting Up - Holding the backward direction on your controller or joystick allows you to simply get up from the ground.
  4. Getting Up in Place - Holding the upward direction enables you to get up while remaining in the same spot. Additionally, by holding up, you can get up using either of the two kick buttons.
  5. Rolling Forward - Holding the forward direction and pressing the button for a roll enables you to roll forward and quickly recover. This technique can be useful for creating offensive opportunities.
  6. Rolling Back - Similarly, holding the backward direction and pressing the roll button executes a backward roll. This move can be strategic for evading attacks and creating space between you and your opponent.
  7. Rolling Left or Right - Hitting the left punch button initiates a roll away from the camera, while using the right punch button performs a roll towards the camera. These rolls help you control your positioning during recovery.
  8. Handspring Attack - By simultaneously pressing both kick buttons after rolling forward, you perform a handspring attack. This move catches your opponent off guard and allows for quick retaliation.
  9. Toe Tap - Holding down and pressing one of the kick buttons executes a fast toe tap. This move can be effective as a surprise attack during recovery.

Recovery Techniques from Stomach Position#

In certain situations, your character may end up on their stomach, which limits your recovery options. However, there are still a few techniques you can utilize:

  1. Getting Up - Holding back allows you to get up when lying on your stomach, just like when you’re on your back.
  2. Getting Up in Place - By holding up, you can get up from the stomach position while remaining stationary.
  3. Rolling Out of the Camera - Holding the left punch button initiates a roll away from the camera when you’re lying on your stomach.
  4. Rolling Into the Camera - On the other hand, pressing the right punch button executes a roll towards the camera in the same position.
  5. Rolling Forward - The forward roll technique is still available even when you’re on your stomach, allowing for offensive recovery options.
  6. Kick Attacks - While the kick attacks from the stomach position are slower and less effective, they can still be used to catch your opponent off guard.

Techniques When Hitting the Ground#

In addition to recovery options after being knocked down, timing your movements right as you hit the ground can provide advantages. Consider the following techniques:

  1. Kip Up - By tapping forward as you hit the ground, you can perform a Kip Up move reminiscent of Shawn Michaels. This move quickly gets you back on your feet.
  2. Rolling Out or Into the Camera - Similar to the previous techniques, hitting the punch button executes rolls in the corresponding directions upon hitting the ground.
  3. Getting Up in Place - Pressing the back button immediately after hitting the ground allows for a quick recovery while staying in the same spot.

Conclusion#

In conclusion, Tekken 7 can be an overwhelming game for newcomers, particularly when it comes to knockdown recovery techniques. Understanding the options available to you when knocked down is crucial for turning the tide of battle and gaining an advantage over your opponent. By mastering the techniques outlined in this guide, you will be well-equipped to handle the chaotic nature of knockdown situations. Practice these strategies, experiment with different combinations, and above all, have fun exploring the rich and complex world of Tekken 7! Thank you for reading.
