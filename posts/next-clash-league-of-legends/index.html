Introduction#

Welcome to Clash, the competitive tournament mode in League of Legends. In this article, we will provide a detailed explanation of how Clash works, from team creation to winning trophies. Whether you’re a seasoned player or new to the game, this guide will help you navigate through the exciting world of Clash. So grab your teammates, prepare for battle, and let’s dive into the thrilling realm of Clash in League of Legends.

Key Points#

  1. Clash is a tournament mode in League of Legends where teams compete against each other for two days to win trophies.
  2. Your team’s qualification for Clash is determined by your ranked placements and skill level.
  3. Team creation opens a few days before the Clash begins.
  4. All team members must lock in before matches begin to secure their slot in the tournament.
  5. The scouting phase allows you to see your opponents’ statistics and champion preferences.
  6. Champions are fully unlocked in Clash, allowing for better coordination during the pick phase.
  7. Each day of Clash is a separate tournament, and you can participate in either or both days with your team.
  8. Clash tournaments use an eight-team bracket format.
  9. If you lose a game, you are eliminated from the tournament in single-elimination brackets.
  10. Even if you lose your first match, your team still earns rewards in consolation brackets and can participate in the second tournament.
  11. To enter Clash, you need a ticket, which can be purchased with blue essence or RP.
  12. Winning matches earns you victory points, which unlock Clash rewards such as team logos and in-game banners.
  13. At the end of the tournament, a Clash orb or capsule will drop, rewarding you with additional prizes.
  14. Tournament winners receive top prizes and trophies, which appear at the Nexus in your Summoner’s Rift games for two weeks.

Step-by-Step Process#

Step 1: Team Creation and Qualification#

To participate in Clash, your team must qualify by having ranked placements and a certain skill level. Team creation opens a few days before the Clash begins. Ensure that your team meets the requirements and everyone is ready to compete.

Step 2: Locking in for Matches#

Before the matches begin, every member of your team must lock in, securing their slot in the tournament. Failure to lock in may result in losing your team’s participation in the Clash.

Step 3: Scouting Phase#

After the lock-in phase, the scouting phase begins. During this phase, you can view your opponents’ statistics and champion preferences. However, your opponents can also view your information. Use this phase strategically to gather information and prepare your team for optimal performance.

Step 4: Pick Phase and Coordination#

In Clash, all champions are unlocked, allowing for better coordination during the pick phase. Coordinate with your teammates to form a well-balanced team composition that suits your strategies and counters your opponents’ choices.

Step 5: Participating in the Tournaments#

Clash consists of separate tournaments held on two consecutive days. You have the option to participate in either or both days with your team. Utilize your skills and teamwork to navigate through the eight-team bracket and advance towards the championship trophy.

Step 6: Single-Elimination Brackets and Consolation Brackets#

In Clash, the tournaments follow a single-elimination bracket format. If your team loses a game, you are out of the running for the championship. However, even if you are defeated in your first match, your team still earns rewards in consolation brackets. This gives you the opportunity to regroup and try again in the second tournament.

Step 7: Tickets and Rewards#

To enter Clash, you need a ticket. Basic tickets can be purchased with blue essence, while premium tickets require RP. Winning matches accumulates victory points, which unlock various Clash rewards such as new team logos and in-game banners. At the end of the tournament, a Clash orb or capsule will drop, granting additional prizes.

Step 8: Trophies and Recognition#

Tournament winners in Clash receive top prizes and trophies. If your team manages to win all their games in a day, your trophy will appear at the Nexus in your Summoner’s Rift games for the following two weeks. Show off your achievements and let everyone know of your success in Clash.

Conclusion#

Clash is an exhilarating tournament mode in League of Legends that combines strategy, teamwork, and competition. By following these guidelines and understanding the intricacies of Clash, you can lead your team to victory and earn prestigious trophies. So gather your friends, assemble your team, and embark on this thrilling journey where every match counts. Good luck, and we’ll see you on the Rift, ready to clash!
