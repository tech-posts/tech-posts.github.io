IzzyTheEmoJigglypuff is back with more Pokemon BDSP, and in this video, she explores the outcome of catching all the Unown. Unown is a unique Pokemon species known for its various alphabet forms. By catching all 28 Unown, Izzy hopes to uncover any special rewards or interactions in the game. Join her as she embarks on this adventure and discovers what happens when all the Unown are finally caught.

Step 1: Catching the Alphabet#

In Pokemon BDSP, to catch the Unown, you must first capture all the alphabet forms. Izzy explains that after catching all the different letters, new opportunities open up in the game. She mentions two specific NPCs she wants to interact with after catching all 28 Unown.

Step 2: The Ball Capsule Guy#

The first NPC Izzy encounters is the Ball Capsule Guy. This character rewards players with a Ball Capsule for each Unown caught. Izzy wonders if something special might happen when she gives him all 26 alphabet Unown forms she has captured so far. She is excited to find out if obtaining all the Unown will result in an additional reward.

Step 3: Finding the Last Two Unown#

After speaking with the Ball Capsule Guy, Izzy sets out to find the remaining two Unown forms. She shares her desire to have the Fly ability to make the search easier. With determination, she heads towards the cave where the last two Unown reside.

Step 4: Exploring the Cave#

As Izzy explores the cave, she notices that it becomes deeper the more Unown she captures. This discovery intrigues her, and she perseveres in her quest to catch the remaining Unown. She encounters a few unexpected obstacles, such as a battling a Geodude, but her excitement remains high.

Step 5: Capturing the Last Unown Forms#

Finally, Izzy successfully captures the last two Unown forms, which are the punctuation marks - a question mark and an exclamation mark. She expresses admiration for their unique appearance and believes they shouldn’t feel left out because they are punctuation marks instead of alphabet characters.

Step 6: No Rewards, but a Sense of Accomplishment#

Izzy returns to the Ball Capsule Guy to show him the last two Unown she caught. Unfortunately, nothing extraordinary happens upon showing him all 28 Unown, and Izzy concludes that catching all the Unown ultimately results in no special rewards or interactions. However, she feels accomplished and satisfied with her achievement.

Conclusion#

In summary, capturing all the Unown species in Pokemon BDSP does not yield any significant rewards or game-changing effects. It may be seen as a minor accomplishment to complete the Unown collection, but players should not expect any notable in-game benefits. Regardless, Izzy encourages viewers to enjoy the journey and have fun exploring the game. Now, there’s no need to spend time hunting down all the Unown yourself, as this video has revealed the outcome. Take it easy and happy gaming!
